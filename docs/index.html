    Introduction

    I enjoy doing research and often find myself thinking through new questions and ideas. With a background in nursing and some clinical experience, I have a strong interest in contributing to healthcare. That said, my curiosity isn’t limited to just one field. Right now, my work focuses on connecting the advances in computer science—particularly in natural language processing and large language models—with real-world healthcare needs. I’m especially interested in building tools that can support both clinicians and patients. If any of this resonates with you and you’re interested in collaborating, feel free to reach out by email. I’m always open to new ideas and conversations.
