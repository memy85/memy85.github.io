Counting the Stars

In the autumn night sky, I count the stars one by one, remembering those I have loved. A star for my mother, a star for my father, and a star for my brother far away. And a star for my friend, who stays in my memories like the gentle wind of childhood days. As I gaze up at the heavens, I place a star for my love, and another for my endless dreams. Tonight, the countless stars whisper soft secrets to my soul, filling the darkness with longing and hope.
